Short Bio

I am currently a assitant professor at Trustworthy Machine Learning and Reasoning (TMLR) Group, advised by Dr. Bo Han. Before that, I received my B.Eng. degree of Computer Science and Technology (Top-notched Student Program) from Sichuan University in 2021.

My research interests lie in the broad areas of robust and reliable machine learning, like adversarial robustness, learning and inference with Out-of-distribution data, and other topics for building human-aligned trustworthy machine intelligent. I am always open for possible collaborations. Please feel free to drop me an email if there is any suitable ideas or opportunities to discuss.
