I am a Professor at the Institute of Science and Technology for Brain-inspired Intelligence, Fudan University. Before joining Fudan University, I was a Research Scientist working with Prof. Ge Wang at Biomedical Imaging Center, Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute. I received my Ph.D. in machine learning from Fudan University in 2017, under the supervision of Prof. Junping Zhang.

My research interests are in machine learning, computer vision, and medical imaging and analysis. My primary interests are representation learning, generative AI, medical image reconstruction, and brain disease analysis.

单洪明，博士生导师，国家级高层次青年人才计划，上海市扬帆人才计划，复旦大学类脑智能科学与技术研究院教授。博士毕业于复旦大学计算机科学技术学院，随后在美国伦斯勒理工学院生物医学影像中心先后担任博士后和研究科学家。主要研究方向为机器学习、医学成像、图像分析等。先后在Nature Machine Intelligence、Nature Communications、IEEE TPAMI、IEEE SPM、IEEE TMI、IEEE TIP、MedIA、ICCV、CVPR、NeurIPS等国际期刊和会议发表论文100余篇，谷歌学术引用5000余次；论文入选世界人工智能大会青年优秀论文奖、国际高被引论文、中国社会计算会议最佳论文。主持国家自然科学基金重点（海外）、面上、青年项目，上海市自然科学基金等多项科研项目。
