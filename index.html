Short Bio

Qingyong Zhu is an Assistant Professor at the Medical AI Research Centre, Shenzhen Institute of Advanced Technology, Chinese Academy of Sciences. He received his Ph.D. in Computational Mathematics from Xi'an Jiaotong University in 2020, advised by Prof. Jian Sun and Prof. Wei Wang. His research interests include inverse problem solving, mathematical programming, computational imaging, and geometric deep learning.
